How It Works

1

Camera Monitoring

Camera continuously captures real-time video from the environment.

2

Fire Detection

AI model analyzes frames and detects fire using image recognition.

3

Alert Generation

System generates alert when fire is detected with confidence level.

4

Emergency Response

Alert is sent to users and authorities to take immediate action.
